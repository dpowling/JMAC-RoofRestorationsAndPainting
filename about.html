Currently Servicing - Gold Coast, Brisbane, Tweed, and Logan

Process and Procedure

  • Cover any surfaces not to be painted
  • All areas washed to remove any mold, salt, dirt and chalky paint
  • Treat any surfaces such as; dry rot, rust, or oxidisation
  • Any bare surfaces such as metal, plastic, cement sheeting, Gyprock and timber will be primed with an appropriate primer
  • Holes and cracks filled using the appropriate product
  • Filler then sealed, and all surfaces given two coats (woodwork will be one coat of undercoat and one coat of gloss. Walls, outdoor timber and ceilings will be given two finish coats).

All jobs are done to the clients request, a home and a business dwelling are two different spaces and there are no limits to the reasons a space is painted.


Each job is unique in nature and we take the care and time to provide a good value quote that suits the needs of the individual dwelling. Please don't hesitate to contact us if you are unsure of a request, and we will get back to you within a reasonable time-frame.
